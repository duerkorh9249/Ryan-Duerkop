My Favorite Recipe

Cookies

Ingrediants

  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softned
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. gather ingrediants in a bowl and mix together with an electric mixer
  3. prep the pan with either spam or a baking sheet
  4. scoop dough onto pan and place into oven
  5. set timer for 10-12 minutes
